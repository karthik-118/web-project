Ram Mandir Concept

The Ram Mandir, also known as the Ayodhya Ram Mandir, is a Hindu temple that is being constructed at the site of the demolished Babri Masjid in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is dedicated to Lord Ram, a prominent deity in Hinduism, and is expected to become one of the largest Hindu temples in the world once completed.
